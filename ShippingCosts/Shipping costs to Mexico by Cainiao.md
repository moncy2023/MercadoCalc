The cost you pay for shipping products from China and Hong Kong to Mexico **depends on your product’s gross weight and whether its price is below or above the free shipping threshold of MXN 299** for Mexico.

Please follow these shipping conditions for every order:

- You can send up to 5 units per order.
- Make sure you declare accurate weight and dimensions. Discrepancies may result in extra charges or account suspension. [Check how to calculate](https://global-selling.mercadolibre.com/help/22213 "Check how to calculate") a product’s gross weight.
